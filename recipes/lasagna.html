Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the first four ingredients;
  2. Spread on four slices of bread. Layer with bacon, tomato and cheese;
  3. Top with remaining bread.
  4. In a large skillet or griddle, melt 2 tablespoons butter.
  5. Toast sandwiches until lightly browned on both sides and cheese is melted, adding butter if necessary.
